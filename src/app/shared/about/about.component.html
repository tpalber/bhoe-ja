About BHOE JA

Explore all the latest Tibetan news while you sip your bhoeja.
Bhoe Ja aims to make it easier to stay informed on the latest Tibetan news from many different sources across the web. Bhoe Ja also allows journalists to get their news articles out to wider audience that just the single news source.

Approach

Bhoe Ja uses web scraping  to extract news from different Tibetan news sources and combines it all in one area.

Supported news sources

  • Phayul
  • Radio Free Asia
  • Tibet Post
  • Tibet Times
  • Voice Of Tibet
