About BHOE JA

Explore all the latest Tibetan news while you sip your bhoe ja.

Bhoe Ja aims to make it easier to stay informed on the latest Tibetan news from many different sources across the web. We hope to empower journalists by making their content more accessible to wider audience than just the single news source.

Bhoe Ja uses web scraping  to extract news from different Tibetan news sources and combines it all in one area.
